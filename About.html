Something Blue

This is an online archive that celebrates the sentimentality of everyday objects. Inspired by the traditional rhyme in folklore, “something old, something new, something borrowed,
something blue,” the archive highlights the stories behind our possessions, often representing symbols of good luck, prosperity, and love. We invite others to share their old, new, borrowed, and blue treasures.
